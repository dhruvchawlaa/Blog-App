About

My name is Dhruv Chawla and I am a full-time student at Seneca College of Applied Arts and Sciences. I am 19 years old and I was born and raised in New Delhi, India.

After graduating high school in summer 2021, I came to Canada for further studies. I will graduate from Seneca College in the fall of 2024 with an Advanced Diploma in Computer Programming and Analysis. I plan on obtaining a bachelors degree in Computer Science from the University of British Columbia.

I have always had a strong interest in computers since I was a child. I am excited to learn more about the different careers in computers and programming as I continue on with my education.
